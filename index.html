What is BootConf?

BootConf is a one day/one track conference for anyone that is interested in starting a tech business, or wants to improve their understanding of bootstrapping methodology.Visit their website to become a member.

Sessions at BootConf will follow a few general themes:

  • Bootstrap Case Studies: Startups that have built long term sustainability by bootstrapping
  • Bootstrap Methodology: Teaching the fundamentals of bootstrapping a new or existing business
  • Technical bootstrapping: Sessions geared towards programmers that want to bootstrap
